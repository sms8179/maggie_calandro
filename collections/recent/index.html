Recent

This past January I went to New York for the first time and while I was there I saw Chicago, one of my favorite movies, on Broadway. In the play the performers wear lingerie the whole time and in the movie they wear fun party dresses. I was inspired by the different portrayals of this story and decided to cross the costumes from both the play and the movie. In this story these women are very powerful and manipulative and the entire time they are getting what they want they look sexy and fabulous. I wanted to fit this attitude of these women in with my garments by making it lingerie that can be worn out in public. During the twenties, which is when the story is set, women began to make a name for themselves and started to break the rules a little bit. They become more and more risqué but still kept it classy. That’s exactly what I wanted to portray this collection as.
