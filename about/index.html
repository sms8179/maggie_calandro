About

Hello! My name is Maggie Calandro and I am currently a senior at LSU majoring in Textile Science, Apparel Design, and Merchandising with a concentration in Apparel Design.

I will graduate in December 2016. I describe my fashion aesthetic as feminine, unique, and fun. I like to challenge myself a lot and work with different mediums. I get my inspiration from current events going on in my life or recent things that catch my eye and then translate it into a garment. I aspire to create beautiful garments for all types of women and aim to have my garments make women feel sexy and confident. I am very interested in costume design and am aiming for a career in that area, specifically Mardi Gras costumes.

In the past I have interned with Carol Guion, a local costume designer for Mardi Gras balls. This summer I will be working in the Costume Department at the Theatre of Baton Rouge.
